Hard Days
You Made It Through Today
▾

For when the day was heavy and you don't know why.  ·  ▶ Listen on YouTube

Hey. I'm glad you're here. I don't know what today looked like for you. Maybe things went wrong in ways you couldn't control. Maybe you said something you regret, or something was said to you that hurt more than it should have. Maybe nothing dramatic happened at all — you just felt heavy all day, and you don't even know why. And somehow that makes it worse. Whatever it was — it's okay. You don't have to explain it. You made it through today. Do you realize that? However messy, however painful, however exhausting — you got through every single minute of it. And you're still here. That matters more than you know. Bad days have a way of making everything feel permanent. Like this heaviness is just... who you are now. Like tomorrow will be exactly the same. But that's the exhaustion talking. That's not the truth. The truth is — you are not your worst day. You are not the mistake you made, or the thing you couldn't finish, or the way you broke down when you didn't want to. You are someone who is trying. Even on the days when trying looks like just getting through. So tonight, I want you to put it all down. Just for now. You don't have to fix anything tonight. You don't have to figure anything out. You just have to rest. You are allowed to rest. Tomorrow is a completely different day. A fresh page. And you'll meet it when you get there. But right now — right now you just need to know that you're not alone in this. Someone out there gets it. And someone out there is rooting for you, even on the days you can't root for yourself. You did good today. You really did. Get some rest. And when you wake up tomorrow, remember — you are still here. Still breathing. Still you. And that is everything.
— You're Still Here ✦

Our Story

Why this space exists.

You're Still Here was born from a simple, aching wish — to find a voice that would just say: "I know. I get it. And it's going to be okay."

So many of us go through the hardest moments alone. Not because no one cares — but because it's 3am, or the words won't come, or we don't want to be a burden. We scroll looking for something that feels like a hand on the shoulder. And we don't find it.

This space exists to be that voice. No advice. No fixing. Just warmth, honesty, and the reminder that you are not the only one who has felt this way.

The person behind these words has been there — in the dark, at 3am, looking for something that felt like a hand on the shoulder. They couldn't find it. So they built it instead.

This is for you. All of it. 🤍

"You are not your worst day.
You are someone who is trying."
— You're Still Here
